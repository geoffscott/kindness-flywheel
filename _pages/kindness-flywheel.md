## The Kindness Flywheel

**As AI becomes universally available, technical capability is commoditizing. What remains defensible is how you treat people.**

This is where I explore that thesis—grounded in research, lived experience, and real observation.

### The Core Insight

In the AI era, competitive advantage is shifting from what you build to **how you treat people**:

- **Teams with high trust** iterate 2x faster and make 40% better decisions
- **Organizations that practice genuine care** see 40-50% lower turnover
- **Cultures built on integrity** command 20-30% price premiums and 5-7x lower customer churn
- **Leaders who genuinely see their people** build organizations that compound over time

But this only works if you actually believe it and build for it.
