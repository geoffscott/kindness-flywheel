# Growth Science

## I help leaders build organizations grounded in clarity, trust, and intention

Whether you're navigating AI transformation, scaling operations, or rethinking your competitive strategy—my work starts with a simple insight: **in an age where technology commoditizes, what remains defensible is how you treat people.**

---

## How We Work Together

### 1:1 Executive Coaching
Deep, ongoing work with individual leaders. We clarify what's driving your decisions, surface the patterns that aren't serving you, and build the leadership practice that fits your actual life — not a framework from a book.

### Curated Group Coaching
Small, intentionally composed groups of startup founders and tech executives working through shared challenges together. The value comes from peers who are in it with you — not just a facilitator with a slide deck.

### Executive Retreats
Intensive, in-person sessions designed to create the space that day-to-day leadership doesn't allow. Step back, see clearly, return with intention.

---

## Approach

Through dialogue that helps you see yourself and your situation more clearly — so you can make choices that align with what actually matters to you. My work helps you become conscious of what's driving your decisions so you can lead and live with intention rather than by default.

### Three Principles

1. **Trust as Strategy** — Not as sentiment, but as operational practice. Embedded in hiring, decision-making, feedback, and how you measure success.

2. **Clarity Over Comfort** — The conversations that matter most are often uncomfortable. I help you have them.

3. **Grounded in Reality** — Theory is useful. What matters is what actually works in your specific context with your specific people.
